My Story

Why I Do This Differently

I got into health insurance because I kept seeing the same problem: people paying premiums that hurt — for coverage that didn't actually protect them when it mattered. A doctor visit avoided because of a deductible. A claim denied because of a network restriction. Families spending thousands on a plan they barely understood.

That's not insurance. That's just a monthly bill with a card in your wallet.

"Let's walk through this and make sure you understand what you're actually buying."

— My standard with every client

We shop the whole market. That means looking at every option available to you — ACA marketplace plans, employer coverage, and medically underwritten private PPO plans — and telling you which one actually makes sense for your situation. What works for you, not what looks good on paper.

I work primarily with self-employed individuals, 1099 contractors, small business owners, and high-income households — people who often feel stuck between bad options. Most of them weren't stuck at all. They just hadn't seen everything available.

What You Can Expect From Me

A Real Comparison, Not a Pitch

I pull quotes from every lane and lay them side by side. You'll see exactly what each option costs, covers, and restricts — before you decide anything.

Plain Language Explanations

No jargon. No scripts. I explain deductibles, networks, underwriting, and plan structures the way I'd want someone to explain them to me.

Your Interests, Not a Carrier's

I don't have quotas. I don't have a "preferred" product I push. If the plan you're already on is actually the best option, I'll tell you that too.

How Coverage Performs Under Pressure

I'm more concerned with how a plan holds up when you actually use it than how it markets. That's the only thing that matters when you really need it.
