Community

Local Groups & Business Networks

DC Insurance isn't just located in Middle Tennessee, it's woven into it. Here are the local business communities Denton is actively part of.

Community Involvement

Built on Relationships, Not Cold Calls

Most of DC Insurance's clients come through referrals, from people who know Denton, have worked with him, or met him at a local networking event. That's not an accident. Being embedded in the local business community means the people who refer clients here have seen how Denton operates firsthand. They're not guessing at character.

Below are the groups and organizations Denton is actively involved with across Middle Tennessee. If you're a member of any of these, you already know someone who can vouch for the work.

Networking · Nolensville

BNI, Nolensville Connections

Business Network International · Middle Tennessee (Nashville)

The world's largest referral networking organization. BNI chapters meet weekly and operate on a structured referral-passing model, one member per profession per chapter. Denton holds the health insurance seat in the Nolensville Connections chapter.

BNI Nolensville Connections
Networking · Smyrna

SNAP

Networking For Awesome People. Smyrna Chapter

A free weekly networking group in Smyrna focused on real relationship-building, no dues, no pressure, just local business owners showing up consistently. One of the most active grassroots networking groups in Rutherford County.

networkingforawesomepeople.com
Networking · Nashville

BAR Networking

Building Authentic Relationships

A Nashville-based networking community built around genuine connection over transactional introductions. BAR brings together business owners and professionals who prioritize long-term relationships and quality referrals.

barnetworking.com
Networking · Nashville

BANG

Business Ambassadors Networking Group of Greater Nashville

A Greater Nashville networking group that connects local business owners and entrepreneurs across industries. BANG focuses on community-first referral relationships and collaborative growth throughout the region.

nashvillebusinessambassadors.com
Directory · Tennessee

Front Door TN

Tennessee Local Business Directory

A Tennessee-focused business directory connecting locals with trusted service providers across the state. DC Insurance is listed as a verified local agency, another way residents searching for health insurance help in Tennessee can find a real person to talk to.

frontdoortn.com
Merchants · Smyrna

SIMA

Smyrna Independent Merchants Association

A local association supporting independent businesses in Smyrna, TN. SIMA brings together locally owned businesses to strengthen the Smyrna community and advocate for independent merchants in Rutherford County.

simatn.org
